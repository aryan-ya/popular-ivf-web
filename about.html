About IVF Department

Advanced Fertility & IVF Care
with Compassion in Banaras

At Popular IVF Hospital, Varanasi, we are committed to holistic management of infertile couples with world-class reproductive treatments. Infertility affects millions globally, and in India, its prevalence ranges between 3.9%–16.8%. Our expert specialists diagnose and treat infertility caused by male, female, combined, or unexplained factors with personalized care and advanced technology.

With modern facilities like IUI, IVF, ICSI, Laser-Assisted Hatching, and Blastocyst Culture, we provide complete infertility workup and effective solutions for conditions like PCOD, endometriosis, fibroids, recurrent implantation failure, endometrial adhesions, and genital tuberculosis. Our dedicated counsellors support couples throughout the journey, making the dream of parenthood possible.
