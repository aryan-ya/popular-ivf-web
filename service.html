We provide advanced

At Popular IVF, we offer a complete range of assisted reproductive technologies and fertility solutions to help couples achieve their dream of parenthood.

IVF (In Vitro Fertilization)

Advanced treatment where eggs and sperm are combined in the lab to create embryos for transfer.

Learn More

IVF-ET

In Vitro Fertilization with Embryo Transfer to the uterus for successful conception.

Learn More

ICSI

Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection – a single sperm is injected directly into an egg.

Learn More

IUI

Intrauterine Insemination where prepared sperm is directly placed in the uterus.

Learn More

TESA

Testicular Sperm Aspiration – sperm retrieved directly from testicular tissue.

Learn More

MESA

Microsurgical Epididymal Sperm Aspiration – retrieval of sperm from epididymis.

Learn More

PESA

Percutaneous Epididymal Sperm Aspiration – minimally invasive sperm retrieval technique.
